Track your farming income and expenses effortlessly!

Features

Income and Expense Tracking

This feature helps farmers keep track of their daily earnings and expenses efficiently.

  • Record and categorize income sources and expenses.
  • View financial summaries with graphs.
  • Generate monthly and yearly financial reports.
  • Export data as CSV or PDF for offline usage.

Loan and Subsidy Management

This feature helps farmers manage their loans and apply for subsidies efficiently.

  • Check and apply for government loan schemes.
  • Monitor loan repayment schedules.
  • Get alerts for upcoming due dates.
  • Compare various loan and subsidy programs.

Data Visualization with Graphs

This feature allows farmers to analyze their financial data with interactive graphs.

  • Generate real-time income and expense graphs.
  • View financial insights with bar, line, and pie charts.
  • Track month-wise and year-wise financial trends.
  • Export graphical reports as PDF or PNG.

Offline Mode Support

Track your financial records even without an internet connection.

  • Work offline without losing any data.
  • Auto-sync data when the internet is available.
  • Store financial records securely on the device.
  • Get a seamless experience without interruptions.
